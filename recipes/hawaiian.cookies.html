Hawaiian Wedding Cake Cookies Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped, toasted macadamia nuts
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped, candied pineapple
  • Confectioners' sugar, in a small bowl to roll cookies in

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a small bowl, cream the butter, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla until light and fluffy
