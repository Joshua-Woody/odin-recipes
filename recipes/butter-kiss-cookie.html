Butter Kiss Cookies Recipe

Description

Flavorful cookies that crumble into your mouth, combined with the sweetness of peanut butter and chocalate.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoon milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 45 chocalate kiss candies

Steps

  1. Set the oven rack to the center position. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. If you are using only one cookie sheet, all the sheet to cool between batches.
  3. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl. Whisk it together to combine it.
  4. In a large bowl, cream together butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and light brown sugar on medium, for 1 minute.
  5. Add egg, milk, and vanill, then mix on medium speed until well blended, about 30 seconds.
  6. Gradually add flour mixture, mixing until combined on low speed, about 1 minute. If the dough is very soft, refrigerate for 30 minutes before rolling.
  7. Measure out 1 leveled tablespoon of cookie dough, then roll dough into approximately 1-inch sized balls.
  8. Roll cookies in sugar and place 2 inches apart on the baking sheet. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly browned, puffed, with some cracks on the surface.
  9. Immediately after, firmly press chocalate into the center of each cookie. The cookie will crack more around the edges.
  10. Cool for 2 minutes on the sheet pan, then tranfer to a cooling rack.
